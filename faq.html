Frequently asked questions

Do I need to supply a credit card for the free trial?

You can sign up for a free two week trial without any payment info. Just submit your email address above and you'll get the next issue of The Sizzle around 5PM.

Will I be automatically charged money when I sign up?

After the free first fortnight, you'll get an email desperately begging for you to continue the subscription. If you like The Sizzle, you can click the link in the request email and you'll be taken to a payment form that will automatically charge your card AU$5/month or $50/year. No payment info is required for a free trial!

I want to skip the trial and pay you now!

Brilliant - use the paid subscription form and we can start a long and beautiful relationship.

Is there an email on weekends/public holidays?

No emails on weekends and Victorian public holidays. I normally take a 2 week break over the Christmas/New Year holiday.

Can I share the email with friends?

There's nothing stopping you forwarding the email to your mates. It'd be nice if they paid too though. This is my primary source of income.

How do I change my billing from monthly to yearly?

Send me an email with the email address where you receive your monthly invoices. Sorry it's a manual process, but I'll respond ASAP!

Need to update your credit card info, cancel your subscription or where payment receipts are sent?

Visit the Moonclerk Payer Portal - you can do all that stuff there. Let me know if you get stuck.
